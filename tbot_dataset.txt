вопрос: What is cyberbullying and how does it manifest?  ответ: Cyberbullying is the use of digital technologies to harass, intimidate, or persecute a person. It manifests through insults, threats, spreading false information, posting humiliating photos or videos, impersonating another person to cause harm, and sharing personal information without consent.
вопрос: How to identify content containing cyberbullying?  ответ: Content containing cyberbullying can be identified by messages with threats, insults, or humiliations, photos or videos showing a person in an unflattering light, false accusations or rumors, publications causing psychological discomfort or fear, and persistent messages.
